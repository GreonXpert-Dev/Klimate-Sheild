What is Green Building?

01
Sustainable ConstructionCreates structures that are environmentally responsible and resource-efficient throughout a building's entire life-cycle: from design and construction to operation and deconstruction.
02
Resource PreservationPreserves raw materials, energy, and water while fighting global warming by reducing greenhouse gas emissions at every stage.
03
Waste & Pollution MinimisationMinimizes waste and pollution across the building lifecycle to reduce environmental impact on surrounding ecosystems.
04
Life-Cycle PerformanceMaximizes long-term performance while ensuring occupant health, comfort, and well-being at every procurement stage.
