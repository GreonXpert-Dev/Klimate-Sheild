Sustainability Framework

Environmental, Social & Governance

ESG is a framework used to evaluate a company's sustainability and ethical impact helping businesses assess risks and opportunities beyond financial performance.

The Three Pillars of ESG

E

Environmental

How a company impacts the natural world carbon emissions, energy efficiency, resource usage, waste management, and climate change initiatives.

  • 1Carbon Footprint Reduction
  • 2Renewable Energy Transition
  • 3Waste & Water Management
  • 4Climate Risk Assessment
S

Social

How a company engages with people employees, customers, and communities. Covers diversity, labour rights, human rights, health & safety, and CSR.

  • 1Labour Rights & Diversity
  • 2Community Impact & CSR
  • 3Health, Safety & Wellbeing
  • 4Human Rights Due Diligence
G

Governance

Corporate leadership and accountability ethical business practices, transparency, risk management, compliance, and board diversity.

  • 1Board Transparency
  • 2Regulatory Compliance
  • 3Risk Management
  • 4Anti-Corruption Policies
