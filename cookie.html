This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how KlimateShield uses them, and your choices regarding cookies. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies
01

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

How We Use Cookies
02

KlimateShield uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous data.

Functional Cookies

Allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced features.

Analytics Cookies

Used to track visitor behavior and improve our website content and services.

Third-Party Cookies
03

Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services such as Google Analytics. These third parties may use cookies to track your activity across multiple websites. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing the respective privacy policies of these third parties.

Managing Cookies
04

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
